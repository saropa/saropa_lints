## TODOs & Hacks

Scan the entire workspace for `TODO`, `HACK`, `FIXME`, and other marker comments.

Results appear in the **TODOs & Hacks** sidebar tree, grouped by tag type or by folder.

- **Tag grouping** — see all TODOs together, all HACKs together
- **Folder grouping** — see markers organized by directory structure
- **Click to navigate** — jump directly to the comment in the editor

Enable workspace-wide scanning to find markers across all project files, not just open ones.
