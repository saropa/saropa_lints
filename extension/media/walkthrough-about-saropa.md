## About Saropa

**Built for Resilience. Designed for Peace of Mind.**

Established in 2010, **Saropa Pty Limited** is a technology firm rooted in financial services and online security. We build digital safeguards — software that cuts through noise, manages risk automatically, and never loses your data.
